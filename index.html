Frequently Asked Questions

What tank materials are recommended for methanol storage?

Carbon steel with appropriate coatings, or stainless steel (316L) for long-term storage. Aluminum and copper alloys should be avoided.

How do I calculate the required ventilation rate?

Minimum 6 air changes per hour for enclosed areas. Use vapor generation rates and dispersion modeling for specific calculations.

What are the key process control parameters?

Temperature (220-280¬∞C), pressure (50-100 bar), H‚ÇÇ/CO ratio (2:1), and catalyst activity monitoring are critical.

How often should safety systems be tested?

Gas detection systems monthly, pressure relief valves annually, and emergency shutdown systems quarterly.
